Saturday RRHS 10am-2pm

Parade and Time Capsule

Parade along Hamilton Street celebrating our 100th and the Cornerstone time capsule reveal. Cornerstone ceremony assisted by Masons - A time capsule was cemented by the Masons in 1921 in the northern-most corner of the RRHS senior building. We've waited 100 years. You do NOT want to miss the unveiling of the time capsule. The Masons will be on hand to assist in the ceremony of our 100-year-old capsule. The Masons will perform a 100 Year Re-dedication ritual.

Saturday gala 7p-11pm

Buy Tickets
Sponsored by RRHS Alumni & Friends Association. You do not want to miss this once-in-a-hundred-years gala event. Destined to be the talk-of-the-town. Cost is $55 per person. You may purchase tickets using the button above.

Dine on heavy hors d'oeuvres as you listen to music and enjoy the company of fellow alums. Be sure to bid on some fantastic items at our silent auction and purchase 50/50 raffle tickets. The proceeds will benefit the RRHS Alumni & Friends Association and some of our RRHS student organizations.

Event will be both inside and outside. Large tents will be outside the Civic Center for extra areas for guest to comfortably socialize.

Treasure Hunt - $1000 prize

Treasure Hunt sponsored by Roanoke Rapids Alumni & Friends Association. City-wide treasure hunt. A gold-colored dog tag necklace has been hidden in the Roanoke Rapids area as the treasure. The finder of the treasure wins $1000 from the Alumni Association. A riddle is provided in increments to help you find the necklace. The first clue of the riddle will be released Friday Sept 24th at 8am. Additional clues of the riddle will be released incrementally on a schedule. The treasure is NOT on school property. The treasure is on publicly accessible property. More information is available at:

rrhstreasure.com
